Tessera

A comprehensive open-source voxel game developed over several years. Built from the ground up using Java and LWJGL (OpenGL), the project features a custom rendering pipeline and complex world-generation algorithms.

Features include:
  • Systems: LAN Multiplayer, Day/Night cycles, and Random Tick Events.
  • Lighting: Real-time Torch and Sun light propagation logic.
  • Physics: Fire spreading mechanics and falling gravity-affected blocks (Sand/Gravel).
  • Agriculture: Dynamic crop growth and Amethyst crystal maturation systems.
  • Exploration: Procedural unique biomes, cave systems, and ore distribution.
  • Transportation: Functional Minecart rail systems and navigable boats.
  • Fauna: Diverse entity AI (Dogs, Cats, Foxes, Beavers) with biome-specific spawning.
  • Interactions: Complex mount mechanics for Horses and Mules requiring saddle items.

Calorie Coach

An AI-Powered web application designed to simplify calorie counting! Built on Node.js, Enter what you ate and let AI do the rest!

  • Simple food tracking with AI: Leverages the Gemini API to parse text into individual food entries. Gemini will use existing entries from the food database, or create new food entries with estimated nutritional data when no relavant database matches are found.
  • Global food database: The app combines disparate data sources into a unified, user-editable food database. This database is public and grows with every new food you eat.
  • Day-by-day tracking: The app records total calories eaten every day. You can easily view how you accomplished your calorie goals on a daily and weekly basis.
